CASE STUDIES

Our expertise includes leadership roles on strategic initiatives across the media, technology and consumer marketing sectors, including digital video, music, sports, sponsorships and advertising, interactive content, publishing, and brand marketing.

Since 2012, we have been partnering with senior leaders in the media, technology, marketing and investment communities to deliver strategic reviews and plans to invest, launch and build new businesses and marketing platforms.

01. COMMERCIAL DUE DILIGENCE FOR AN EQUITY INVESTMENT IN A PREMIER SPORTS PROPERTY

CLIENT: A leading hedge fund with diversified investments, including media, technology and communications.

OUR ROLE: Contemplating an equity investment in a premier sports business, the client enlisted us to evaluate the sponsorship revenue gaps and future upside opportunity.

02. COMMERCIAL DUE DILIGENCE FOR A MINORITY STAKE ACQUISITION IN A LARGE TALENT AGENCY

CLIENT: A leading hedge fund with diversified investments, including media, technology and communications.

OUR ROLE: Contemplating an equity investment in a premier sports business, the client enlisted us to evaluate the sponsorship revenue gaps and future upside opportunity.

03. MARKET ASSESSMENT, BUSINESS PLAN & FINANCIAL VALUATION FOR AN EARLY-STAGE INVESTMENT IN A MOTORSPORTS BUSINESS

CLIENT: A leading media company operating a stable of cable networks and digital media properties.

OUR ROLE: We assisted the C-suite in evaluating an early-stage investment in a motorsports business and its intellectual property, including overall strategic plan, financial model, asset valuation and corporate governance plan.

04. STRATEGIC OPTIONS REVIEW AND GO-FORWARD STRATEGY FOR MID-TIER OTT VIDEO BUSINESS

CLIENT: A large national retailer operating a domestic chain of department stores.

OUR ROLE: We supported the senior leadership team in defining and evaluating strategic options and a go-forward strategy regarding the client’s ownership position in an over-the-top video distributor.

05. SPORTS TECHNOLOGY BUSINESS MODEL DESIGN AND MONETIZATION STRATEGY

CLIENT: Fortune 100 technology company with sports technology investments.

OUR ROLE: We assisted senior leadership in designing the future business model and monetization strategy for a portfolio of technologies serving major sports leagues, media companies and sports programmers to create and deliver the next generation of immersive sports content and experiences.

06. EXPERIENTIAL ENTERTAINMENT MARKETING INVESTMENT VALUATION FOR FORTUNE 500 BRAND

CLIENT: Fortune 500 financial services brand with a large experiential marketing budget and network of media assets across sports, music, film and fashion.

OUR ROLE: We worked in conjunction with the client’s agency network advising the senior marketing leadership on several media and technology opportunities, including investments in television productions, musical artist collaborations, live music streaming, and fashion and sports partnerships.
